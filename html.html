Here are the top seven reasons why people flock to a Barbering career.

  1. 1. BARBERING IS NOT A NINE-TO-FIVE JOB

    Does the idea of sitting at a desk all day stress you out? Do you cringe at the thought of clacking away at a computer or pushing paper around an office? Then barbering may be just the career for you. You work with clients in a creative environment, and no two days in a barbershop are ever the same.

  2. YOU CAN EXPRESS YOUR FLAIR FOR CREATIVITYB

    The barbershop is a creative environment. There are always new styles and techniques to learn. You can even invent some yourself. Do you want to try a new fade? Work on your beard styling skills? Let your creative side shine!

  3. BARBERS CAN BE SOCIAL

    You won’t be stuck in a cubicle all by yourself. Making good conversation as a Barber is part of the job. It makes the time fly faster for both you and your clients, and it’s just great customer service. By getting to know your clients, you make yourself feel more like their friend than just someone they pay for a service. This means they’re more likely to come back to you, and not another barber.

  4. YOU HAVE FLEXIBILITY AS A BARBER

    Many barbers are self-employed, which means they can set their own hours. Self-employed barbers also handle their own marketing and set their own rates. If you work on commission, you won’t have as much flexibility with your time, but you also won’t have to worry about marketing or scheduling appointments—someone else will take care of that for you, so you have more time to focus on barbering.
